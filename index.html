Open Your Account — Terms and Conditions

1. Account Opening

By opening an account, you agree to provide accurate and truthful information. We reserve the right to verify the information provided and may refuse to open an account at our discretion.

2. Deposits and Withdrawals

Minimum deposit requirements may apply. Withdrawals may be subject to notice periods and limitations as per the specific account type chosen.

3. Interest Rates

Interest rates are subject to change without prior notice. The current interest rates will be displayed on our website and at our branches.

4. Fees and Charges

Account maintenance fees, transaction fees, and other charges may apply. Please refer to our current fee schedule for details.

5. Account Closure

We reserve the right to close your account with reasonable notice if it is not maintained in accordance with our policies.
