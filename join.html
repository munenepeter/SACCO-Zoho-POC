Terms & Conditions

1. Membership

By joining Redian Sacco, you agree to uphold the cooperative's bylaws and contribute regularly. Membership is open to Kenyan citizens and residents aged 18 and above.

2. Contributions

A minimum monthly contribution of KSh 500 is required. Failure to contribute for three consecutive months may result in account suspension pending review.

3. Loans

Loan eligibility is based on your savings balance and creditworthiness. Loans are subject to approval by the credit committee. You may borrow up to 3× your savings balance.

4. Dividends

Dividends are declared annually at the AGM based on the Sacco's financial performance. The indicative rate is 14% p.a. Past rates do not guarantee future returns.

5. Data Privacy

Your personal data is processed in accordance with Kenya's Data Protection Act 2019. We will never sell your data to third parties without consent except where required by law.

6. Account Closure

You may withdraw membership by giving 90 days written notice. All outstanding loans must be settled before membership is terminated.
