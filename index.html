Terms & Conditions

1. Account Opening

By opening an account, you agree to provide accurate and truthful information. We reserve the right to verify the information provided and may refuse to open an account at our discretion.

2. Deposits and Withdrawals

Minimum deposit requirements may apply. Withdrawals may be subject to notice periods and limitations as per the specific account type chosen.

3. Interest & Dividends

Dividend rates are subject to approval at the AGM. The current indicative rate is 14% p.a. on deposits. Rates may vary year to year.

4. Fees and Charges

Account maintenance fees, transaction fees, and other charges may apply. Please refer to our current fee schedule available at any branch or on our website.

5. Data Protection

Your personal data is handled in accordance with the Kenya Data Protection Act 2019. We will never sell your data to third parties.
